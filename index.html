Start-up Stage

What stage is your Start-up?

Stage 1

Acceleration

This is the very beginning of the Startup lifecycle, before your start up is even officially in existence. You've got your business idea and you are ready to take the first steps as a founder.

Stage 1
Acceleration
At this stage, you’ve entered the process of scaling up. You’re able to serve more than a small batch of customers, adding more employees to your company is easier, and you have a good financial record and documentation to back up your business 
Stage 2
Ideation
At this stage, you’re still primarily generating ideas surrounding your business model, your product or service, what customer base you want to serve and generally developing the company.
Stage 3
Traction
At this stage, you’ve already begun selling your product or service, and are gaining both noticeably increased numbers of clients, as well as more insights into how to scale up your operations. 
Stage 4
Validation
At this stage, you have a more or less firm grip on what you want your product to be, what customers you’re going to serve, and how your business model will ensure value to clients. You’re talking to potential customers, making your idea more valid, and are ready to start setting up some revenue streams.
